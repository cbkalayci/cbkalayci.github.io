Projects

Selected academic projects and applied research streams

Current Directions

  • Rich Vehicle Routing and Sustainable Logistics: Metaheuristic and hybrid optimization models for real-world routing systems with compartment, energy, and service constraints.
  • Disassembly Line Balancing in Circular Manufacturing: Decision support methods for partial disassembly planning and recovery-oriented production systems.
  • AI-Assisted Combinatorial Optimization: Learning-based search guidance and data-driven heuristics for large NP-hard optimization instances.

Applied Research Themes

  • Electric Vehicle Logistics: Time-window constrained two-echelon routing models and practical algorithmic frameworks.
  • Production and Scheduling Analytics: Heuristic solution strategies for industrial planning and scheduling environments.
  • Portfolio and Decision Analytics: Optimization-driven modeling for risk-aware decision support.
