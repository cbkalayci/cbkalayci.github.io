Research Interests

  • Design and Analysis of Optimization and AI Algorithms: development, hybridization, and performance evaluation of metaheuristics and machine-learning techniques for complex decision-making problems.
  • Application Domains: production and disassembly line balancing, scheduling, electric vehicle routing, and portfolio optimization.
  • Methodological Focus: combinatorial optimization, data-driven forecasting, environmentally conscious modeling, and sustainability-driven systems design.
