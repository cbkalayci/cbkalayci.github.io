Research

Research Interests

  • Methodological: metaheuristics, combinatorial optimization, machine-learning-assisted optimization, and quantum-inspired approaches.
  • Application domains: multi-compartment vehicle routing, electric vehicle routing, disassembly line balancing, scheduling, and portfolio optimization.
  • Sustainability: circular manufacturing systems and environmentally conscious decision models.
