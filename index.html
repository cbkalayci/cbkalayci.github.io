Overview

Optimization and AI methods for complex industrial systems.

My work focuses on the design, hybridization, and performance evaluation of optimization and artificial intelligence algorithms for decision-making under operational constraints. Across recent research, I have concentrated on combinatorial optimization, sustainable systems design, city logistics, disassembly line balancing, and machine-learning-enabled decision support.

Research

Current focus areas

Optimization & Metaheuristics

Development of heuristic, hybrid, and neighborhood-based methods for difficult combinatorial optimization problems.

Sustainable Manufacturing

Partial disassembly, circular production systems, and environmentally conscious modeling for industrial decisions.

Routing & Logistics

Electric vehicle routing, city logistics, milk collection, and service-constrained logistics planning.

AI for Decision Support

Data-driven forecasting, machine learning, and quantum-inspired approaches integrated with optimization workflows.
